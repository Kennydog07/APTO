Ready-to-use prompts

Copy. Paste. Done.

Four of the 20 prompts from the free APTO AI Starter Kit. Each one works with ChatGPT's free tier. Just swap the brackets for your details.

Email writing
Follow-up after a meeting
"Write a follow-up email to [client name] about [topic]. Keep it under 80 words. Tone: friendly but direct. End with one clear next step. No filler phrases like 'I hope this finds you well.'"
Works with: ChatGPT free tier
Social media
Instagram caption generator
"Write 3 Instagram caption options for [my business, which does X]. Tone: [professional/friendly/fun]. Include a question to drive comments. Keep each under 150 characters."
Works with: ChatGPT free tier
Customer service
Polite complaint response
"Write a professional reply to this customer complaint: [paste complaint]. Acknowledge the issue, apologise sincerely, offer [solution]. Keep it under 100 words. Do not be defensive."
Works with: ChatGPT free tier
Sales
Proposal introduction
"Write the opening paragraph of a business proposal for [client name] at [company]. We are proposing [service/product] to solve [their problem]. Tone: confident, clear, no jargon."
Works with: ChatGPT free tier
