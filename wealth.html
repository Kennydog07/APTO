The one rule to know

The 50/30/20 rule — explained simply.

One budget framework that works for almost everyone. No spreadsheets. No apps you won't use. Just three numbers to know.

50%
Needs

Rent or mortgage, food, utilities, transport, insurance. Things you genuinely can't live without.

30%
Wants

Eating out, subscriptions, hobbies, holidays. Things that make life enjoyable but aren't strictly essential.

20%
Savings & Debt

Emergency fund, investments, pension, overpaying on debt. This is your future-self fund.
